about us

Lompasago Sacco Society was registered on 20th June, 1980 by the Commissioner of Co-operative Development, under Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490 section 7 of the Laws of Kenya.

The Society's main aim is to promote the economic interests of members by encouraging thrift through the mobilization of deposits, savings and investments. Since inception, the society has undergone a series of transformation which include introduction of various products and services.

our mission

To improve the socio- economic status of members, customers and community at large through savings mobilization, affordable credit provision, investment and education.

our vision

To be the leading Sacco in the country in off­ering quality financial services to our members and customers

membership

Our membership is drawn from various employers across Nairobi and from Company Branches outside Nairobi. Membership now stands at 3800 members drawn from 170 companies.

our values

To realize our vision and mission, the Sacco recognizes the need to nurture and be guided by certain core values in its business. Lompasago commits itself to:

  • Enhancing equality, equity and solidarity within the co-operative movement.
  • Encouraging practices that are environmental friendly/Social responsibility.
  • Encourage creativity, innovativeness and embrace new ideas
  • Operating on a high level of accountability and transparency.
  • Promoting the concept of self-help and self-responsibility.
  • Co-operation and Teamwork.
